F.A.Q.

  • What's the game about?

    • Andromeda will be a 4x strategy game set in space. The main objective will be managing your empire, trading and fighting with other players. The story is: Humans are colonizing a new galaxy.
    The story is purposely very open ended so that players can create their own stories in game.

  • What will be the main purpose of the game?

    • Your main goal will be to survive.
    The galaxy is full of hostile and malevolent powers, while also being full of curiosities that you will want to explore.
    Survive, thrive, cooperate, and conquer.

  • What kind of rules will apply to players?

    • We have one basic rule: no hacking the game or into other player's accounts.
    Do you want to backstab, lie, steal, raid, create monopolies, or create a galatic empire with your friends? Go for it!

  • What is a 4x game?

    • 4X games usually feature complex simulations of scientific research, economics, diplomacy, and social dynamics. Instead of controlling a single person or ship in space, you will be deciding the fate of an entire civlization

  • What platform will this be on?

    • This game will be browser based, so it will work on anything with an internet connection.
    We do not recomend playing on your smart fridge however
